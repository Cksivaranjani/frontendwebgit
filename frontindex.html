Tamilnadu Map

Salem

Salem is a city in Tamil Nadu known for its significant textile and steel industries, contributing greatly to the state's economy.
It is also famous for mango cultivation and is close to tourist attractions like the Yercaud hill station.

Coimbatore

Coimbatore, often referred to as the "Manchester of South India," is a major industrial hub renowned for its textile, engineering, and automobile industries.
The city boasts numerous educational institutions and is known for its pleasant climate.

Dharmapuri

Dharmapuri is a town characterized by its hilly terrain and agricultural activities in Tamil Nadu.
It is home to the Hogenakkal Falls, a popular tourist destination often called the "Niagara of India."

Dindigul

Dindigul is famous for its lock-making industry, earning it the nickname "Lock City."
The city is also known for the historical Dindigul Fort and its distinctive Dindigul biryani, a cherished local cuisine.

Cuddalore

Cuddalore is a coastal district in Tamil Nadu, is known for its fishing industry and scenic beaches like Silver Beach.
It is a region with rich cultural heritage with ancient temples such as the Pataleeswarar Temple.
